About Us

Mapo Tofu

One of Sichuan cuisine most famous dishes is mapo tofu. This dish features soft tofu cubes in a rich, spicy sauce made with fermented broad bean paste, Sichuan peppercorns, and chili oil. It’s often topped with ground pork and green onions for extra savory goodness.

Fish-flavored shredded pork

Fish-flavored shredded pork is a classic Sichuan cuisine dish. The shredded pork is seasoned with an aromatic blend of ginger, garlic, green onions, Sichuan peppercorn, fermented bean paste, and chili bean paste. It’s a perfect balance of sweet, salty, sour, and spicy flavors.

Hot pot

Hot pot is a popular Sichuan cuisine consisting of a simmering pot of spiced broth, accompanied by raw ingredients such as thinly sliced meats, vegetables, and tofu. It’s perfect for sharing with friends and family, and the spicy and bold flavors are a hallmark of Sichuan cuisine.
